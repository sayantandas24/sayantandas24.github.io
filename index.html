ABOUT US

REunion and Bengal Engineering College Annuals is the annual cultural fest of IIEST, Shibpur, a premier engineering institute of the country. Spread across a span of 4 days, REBECA is a hub of pure ecstasy and talent showcase, with an assortment of performances by students as well as renowned dance and music artists of the nation. Having started off back in 1932, the oldest fest of India isn't just about art and culture, it's about togetherness, reunions, new friendships, memories, emotions and so much more. Having said that, the myriad of cultural and art events, for instance, fashion shows, literary competitions, dance performances, student-alumni matches, band competitions, seminars, etc. ensure that each one can have a diverse experience on the four magical nights of the fest. This year, in 2020, an ever-progressing REBECA will witness its 82nd edition, an iconic tradition to be celebrated with due splendor and exquisite details.
